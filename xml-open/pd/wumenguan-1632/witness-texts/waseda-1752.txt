This witness is a 1752 Japanese woodblock recut of the Mumonkan held at
Waseda University. It was used as a secondary cross-check during the
production of the reading edition — not as a primary source for the body
text.

No independent transcription of this witness exists yet. The witness was
consulted visually during OCR adjudication and leaf-level verification
for cases where the 1632 NDL base witness was ambiguous or damaged.
