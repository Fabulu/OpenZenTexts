This witness is a separate work — Wumen Huikai's Recorded Sayings — not
a direct copy of the Wumenguan. It was cited only as a tertiary
corroborant for Huikai's voice and phrasing, not as a primary authority
for case text or commentary wording.

No independent transcription of this witness exists. It is a 70-page
woodblock print in classical Chinese, confirmed PD-Japan / PD-scan.
